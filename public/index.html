Our Story

On a trip to Rio de Janeiro, finally in a quiet moment away from the other visitors at the Jardim Botânico, Alex had decided it was time.

His heart was beating hard in his chest, he asked her three questions. On the third question, he bent to one knee, and purposed to Camilla in font of Corcovado Mountain. She accepted with some help from her favorite movie, saying, “A thousand times yes”.

9 years, 6 apartments, 5 cities, Camilla & Alex are finally getting married! We can't wait to celebrate our union with our family and friends. So please join us on February 18th of 2017 for an evening of drinks, dinner, and dancing.

Hope to see you there!

The Ceremony - 509 N Florida Ave, Tampa, FL 33602

Sacred Heart Catholic Church was constructed in 1905 in downtown Tampa, Florida and is one of the oldest churches in the city of Tampa. The church is predominantly a Romanesque structure, with a 135-foot dome, solid oak pews and doors, porcelain tiles, and a Carrara marble altar. Its 70 stained glass windows were designed and manufactured for Sacred Heart by Franz Mayer & Co. of Munich, Germany.
